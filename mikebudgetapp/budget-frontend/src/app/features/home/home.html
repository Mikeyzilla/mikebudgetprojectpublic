The Vision

Not sure where your money’s going each month? BudgetBuddy’s here to help you take control — no stress, no complicated stuff.

  • Set your own budget timeline — whether it’s one month or a year — and track your income and spending week by week.
  • Just plug in your basics like income, your state, and how many dependents you have, and BudgetBuddy will give you easy-to-understand suggestions.
  • Think of it as your personal money coach helping you build better habits early on.
